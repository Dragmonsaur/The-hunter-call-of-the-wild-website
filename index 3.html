Hirschfelden

Hirschfelden is in an old war zone in Germany from the second world war thats being turned into a hunting resverve.

Hirschfelden is the first of the 2 starter maps. Hirschfelden has 7 different species animals Red deer, Canada goose, Europeann Bison, Fallow deer, Roe deer, Red fox, Ring neck pheasent, Wild boar and European Rabbit.

In Hirschfelden you can get 4 great ones. Red fox, Ring neck pheasent, Fallow deer, Red deer but is considered one of the worst great one maps in the game since its so bushy.

Laykon lakes

Laykon lakes is a resverve located in the pacific Northwest of America

Laykon lake is the 2nd starting map. Laykon lakes has 9 different species of animals Moose, Black bear, Roosevelt Elk, Blacktail deer, Whitetail deer, Coyote, Mallard, White-tailed Jackrabbit, and Merriam turkey

You can get 3 different Great ones Moose, Black bear and Whitetail deer and is debatably the best Whitetail deer great one map.
